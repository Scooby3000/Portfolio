Attendance Management System Using Face Recognition

  • What its purpose is:
    • Face recognition is one of the most important applications of biometrics based authentication system in the last few decades.
    • So we a group of 3 created a way to manage daily attendance using face recognition.
    • After the user register themselves on the data base they only need to show their face in front of the camera every day after and the attendance will be recorded automatically and the mail is sent to the Teacher automatically also at the end of the day
  • Technologies Used:
    • PYTHON, Open-Cv, Numpy, Pillow, Yagmail
